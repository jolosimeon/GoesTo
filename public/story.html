My Hero

by Lucas Tomas
Sept. 27, 2015
5.4 ☆☆☆☆☆

Grandpa always smiles and always makes me laugh, but I only see him in his happiest when he sings. So one day I asked him why didn't he become a singer since it makes him happy and he is a really good singer. Grandpa said that he used to sing for a living and he was starting to become famous, but the pay wasn't enough to support his family. Eventhough it was really hard for him, he gave up his greatest dream for the people he loved most so he started working all day and all night to provide the needs of his family. I never knew grandpa did something like that for grandma and dad. I just wanna leave my grandpa's story here for anyone to read.

A hero isn't always an epic war general or a soldier who died fighting for his country. He could just be someone right beside you, always smiling, always loving, and always giving his best for the people he loves.
